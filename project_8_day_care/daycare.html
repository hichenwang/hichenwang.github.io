How Hard Is It to Enroll in A Child Care in Ontario?

by Chen Wang  |  April 16, 2018

Ontario has witnessed a steady increase in the need for child care service with the rise in employment rates among women. Accessibility to afford child care service has been becoming an important factor for Canadian residents to choose their homes.

According to 2011 General Social Survey, one-third (33%) of parents regard location as the leading reason behind their choice of child care.
